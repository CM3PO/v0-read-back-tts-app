## Description

**Short Description (30 characters):**
Listen to your notes on the go

**Full Description:**

ReadBack transforms your written notes into spoken words. Save unlimited-length text sections and have them read aloud using high-quality voices whenever you need.

Perfect for:
• Listening to articles while commuting
• Reviewing notes hands-free
• Accessibility and learning support
• Multitasking with your content

KEY FEATURES:
• Unlimited text length per section
• Multiple natural-sounding voices
• Save and organize your sections
• Offline playback support
• Clean, distraction-free interface

FREE PLAN:
• Up to 10 saved sections
• 4 standard voices
• All core features

PREMIUM ($10.99/month):
• Unlimited saved sections
• 2 premium voices
• Priority support

ReadBack makes your text accessible anytime, anywhere. Start listening today!
